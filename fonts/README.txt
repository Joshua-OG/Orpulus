ORPULUS BRAND FONTS
===================

Copy the following files from your brand assets into this folder:

  1. Alumni Sans Pinstripe.ttf
     Source: Logo and Branding/orpulus-brand-zip-file/fonts/Alumni Sans Pinstripe.ttf

  2. Lato.ttf
     Source: Logo and Branding/orpulus-brand-zip-file/fonts/Lato.ttf

The website will fall back to Google Fonts (Alumni Sans + Lato) if local files are not present.
